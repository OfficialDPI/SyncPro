Our Story

Coffee Cove was born from a simple belief: great coffee brings people together. Founded in 2018 by two childhood friends, our downtown shop has become a beloved gathering place for the community.

We source our beans directly from farmers, roast them in-house, and craft every drink with intention. From our cozy seating to our friendly baristas, every detail is designed to make you feel at home.
